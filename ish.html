Our Story

Founded in 1995, Pizza Palace has been serving authentic Italian pizzas made with love and tradition. Our founder, Giovanni Rossi, brought his family recipes from Naples to create the most delicious pizzas in town.

We use only the freshest ingredients, imported Italian flour, and homemade tomato sauce to ensure every bite is a taste of Italy.
